Privacy Policy

TurtleReporter

Last updated: July 14, 2026

1. Definitions

  • Application means the software program provided by the Company downloaded by You on any electronic device, named TurtleReporter.
  • Company (referred to as "the Company", "We", "Us" or "Our") refers to Wave Labs, Funchal, Portugal.
  • Account means a unique account created for You to access our Service.
  • Personal Data is any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
  • Service refers to the Application.
  • You means the individual accessing or using the Service.

The Application is used to record sea turtle sightings for conservation and scientific research purposes.

2. Types of Data Collected

  • Account data: first and last name, email address, phone number (optional) and password, provided upon registration.
  • Location: with Your prior permission, the Device's GPS position is collected to record the route of observation trips and to georeference reported sightings. Location is only collected while a trip is active or when creating a sighting, never in the background without an active trip.
  • Pictures: with Your prior permission, the Application accesses the Device's camera and photo library exclusively to attach images of the sky, the sea and the turtles to sighting reports, and to set a profile picture.
  • Preferences: application language and usage settings.
  • Usage Data: collected automatically when communicating with Our servers, such as the Device's IP address, device model, operating system and access logs, used exclusively for security and diagnostics.

3. Use of Your Personal Data

  • To provide and maintain our Service, including creating and authenticating Your Account.
  • To record and display Your sightings, trips and routes on the map.
  • To support scientific research on sea turtles, using aggregated and georeferenced data.
  • To improve and monitor the functioning of the Service.

We do not sell Your Personal Data, do not share it with business partners, and do not use it for marketing or advertising.

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

Account authentication is managed by Firebase Authentication (Google LLC), which stores Your email and access credentials in encrypted form. All other data — profile, sightings, photographs, trips and GPS positions — is stored on the Company's server, operated by the project team. All communication between the Application and the servers uses secure connections (HTTPS) only.

The Application also uses Google Maps to display maps, subject to Google's privacy policy.

International transfer: Google services (Firebase, Maps) may process data on servers located outside Portugal or the European Union. Such transfers take place under the safeguards provided by the GDPR, including standard contractual clauses.

5. Retention of Your Personal Data

Personal Data is retained for as long as Your Account is active. After Account deletion, Personal Data is removed from Our systems; scientific sighting data may be retained in anonymized form, with no link to Your identity.

6. Account Deletion and Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify and erase Your Personal Data, and to withdraw consent at any time.

You can delete Your Account directly in the Application: Profile → Delete Account. This removes Your authentication account (Firebase) and all associated Personal Data on Our server.

Alternatively, You may request Account deletion or exercise any other right by contacting Us using the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Device Permissions

  • Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION): trip route recording and sighting georeferencing.
  • Camera (CAMERA): taking photographs for reports.
  • Photo library (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES): selecting existing photographs to attach to reports or the profile.

All permissions are requested at the moment the corresponding feature is used and can be revoked at any time in the Device settings.

8. Disclosure and Security

Law enforcement: We may disclose Personal Data if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities, to protect rights, the safety of users, or to prevent wrongdoing in connection with the Service.

Security: We apply appropriate technical measures (encryption in transit, access control), but no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and We cannot guarantee its absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If You are a parent or guardian and You are aware that Your child has provided Us with Personal Data, please contact Us.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update Our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify You of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.
